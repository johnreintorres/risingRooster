About Rising Roosters

As a design agency, we decided to come up with an imaginary bastketball team as a brand study. The exercise allowed us to explore different elements from logo design to typography to uniforms and merchandise design.

01

INTRODUCTION

Rising Roosters is an in-house basketball team from a design agency in the Philippines.

It is our goal to diversify our creativity into an interesting twist of branding.

The brand study began with selecting a team name and an icon.

The decision to use roosters stemmed from the cockfighting culture in the Philippines.

To communicate potential and promise, we decided to call the team Rising Roosters.

We made a number of logo studies and chose to adopt a simple, flat design for the final version.
Using flat design helps us translate the logo easily to different applications and surfaces.
It also gives a strong, clean look to the brand. We likewise used dark, rich colors to convey brand presence.
